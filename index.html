The Story Behind Smart Scroll

A simple solution to a common challenge

Why Smart Scroll Exists

Smart Scroll was born from a simple observation:
Volume knobs are incredibly satisfying to use, but they're limited to just one function.

As someone who spends hours coding, reading, and navigating long documents,
I wanted to repurpose this tactile control for something I do hundreds of times a day — Scrolling.

Who It's For

Smart Scroll was designed with various users in mind:

  • Developers  who navigate through extensive codebases
  • Writers and editors  who work with long-form content
  • Researchers  who read through academic papers and documentation
  • Productivity enthusiasts  looking to optimize their workflow

Frequently Asked Questions

What devices work with Smart Scroll?

+

Smart Scroll works with any device that has media volume controls, including keyboards with volume knobs, media keyboards, and external volume controllers.

Will Smart Scroll affect my normal volume controls?

+

No, Smart Scroll only intercepts volume controls when in an active scrolling mode.
Your volume controls work normally at all other times.

How do I switch between modes?

+

You can switch modes using the WIN+Shift+F1 hotkey or through the system tray icon.

Is Smart Scroll open source?

+

Yes, Smart Scroll is an open-source project. You can view the source code and contribute on GitHub.
